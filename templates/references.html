How to Use These References

Research Process
  • Start with the Constitution to understand the current framework
  • Check the Declaration of Independence for founding principles
  • Review relevant amendments for your specific challenge
  • Study Supreme Court cases that interpret constitutional text
Writing Tips
  • Use precise, clear language like the original Constitution
  • Consider unintended consequences of your proposed changes
  • Address specific mechanisms rather than vague principles
  • Think about enforcement and implementation
